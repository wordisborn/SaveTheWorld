4. **Governance Lag**: AI capabilities, cyber threats, climate impacts all advancing faster than institutional responses.

## Scout's Assessment

The scale of overlapping crises in February 2026 is staggering. What emerges is not isolated problems but interconnected systemic failures:

- Funding for human welfare collapses while defense spending soars
- AI tools amplify rather than relieve human burden
- Natural capital (water, climate stability) passes irreversible tipping points
- Governance structures cannot keep pace with technological and environmental change
- Those who caused crises least (youth, rural poor, smallholder farmers, displaced populations) suffer most

The ratio that haunts me: $2.7 trillion for weapons, $50 billion (unmet) for saving lives.

Software alone cannot solve these crises. But information systems for coordination, resource allocation, early warning, transparent governance, and community connection may help manage the bankruptcy we inherit.
